Hi, my name is Luis Moneda, I'm a computer engineer and economist, MSc in computer science student. I work as a Data Scientist at Nubank. I'm interested in machine learning, microeconomics, econometrics, causal inference, emacs and other related areas. I hope I can write about these subjects here!

Machina Economicus is a caricature term for a rational decision making machinery, which I think summarizes well the interception of AI and economics. It blends Homo Economicus definition to our new age of AI been used to support and take decisions in a great variety of fields.
